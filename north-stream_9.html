North Stream Supply is the procurement and logistics division of NorthStream Group — an Abuja-based defense and government holding company. We bring the Group's deep analytical expertise directly into the supply chain.

We combine rigorous compliance standards with a commitment to speed — bridging the gap between complex procurement requirements and the operational urgency our clients face on the ground. Whether defense, government, or commercial, every engagement is handled with the same accountability.

As a new company, we bring fresh perspective, modern processes, and a long-term commitment to building trust through every engagement — one contract at a time.
